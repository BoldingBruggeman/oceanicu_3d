Satellite products, in-situ observations, and reanalysis data used for model validation.

Satellite Data#

Sea Surface Temperature (SST)#

  • NOAA OISST: Daily, 0.25° resolution
  • CMEMS L4: Daily, multiple sensors

Sea Surface Salinity (SSS)#

  • SMOS: ESA, L3, 9-day, 25km
  • SMAP: NASA, Level 3, 8-day, 70km

Sea Surface Height (SSH)#

  • CMEMS Altimetry: Multi-mission gridded product
  • AVISO: Jason, Sentinel missions

In-Situ Observations#

Argo Floats#

  • Source: Global Argo Program
  • Variables: Temperature, Salinity profiles
  • Coverage: Global ocean

Tide Gauges#

  • GESLA v3: Global Extreme Sea Level Analysis
  • PSMSL: Permanent Service for Mean Sea Level
  • UHSLC: University of Hawaii Sea Level Center

Tidal Products#

FES2014#

  • Resolution: 1/16° (~7km)
  • Constituents: 34 major and minor
  • Coverage: Global

TPXO9#

  • Resolution: 1/30° (~3.7km)
  • Constituents: 15 primary
  • Coverage: Global with Arctic

Atmospheric Forcing#

ERA5#

  • Source: ECMWF Reanalysis
  • Resolution: 0.25° hourly
  • Variables: Wind, pressure, heat fluxes, precipitation

Data Access#

All data sources are publicly available. Contact the data providers for access details.
