Methodology#

Horizontal Validation#

Model surface fields compared against gridded satellite products and in-situ observations.

Statistics computed:

  • RMSE: Root Mean Square Error
  • Bias: Mean difference (Model - Observations)
  • MAE: Mean Absolute Error
  • Correlation: Pearson correlation coefficient

Spatial statistics show error distribution across the domain.
