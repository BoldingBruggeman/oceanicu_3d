Ranking Methodology#

Models are ranked using a weighted composite score:

S = 0.40 × (RMSE / σ_obs) + 0.20 × (|Bias| / σ_obs) + 0.20 × (MAE / σ_obs) + 0.20 × (1 − r)

where σ_obs is the observed standard deviation. Normalising by σ_obs makes the score dimensionless and comparable across variables with different units.

MetricWeightRationale
RMSE / σ_obs40 %Sensitive to large errors; dominant skill indicator
|Bias| / σ_obs20 %Systematic offset; important for applications
MAE / σ_obs20 %Robust to outliers; complements RMSE
1 − r20 %Phase/pattern errors not captured by magnitude metrics

Reference thresholds (normalised units):

RatingScore
Good≤ 0.17
Acceptable≤ 0.34
Poor> 0.34
